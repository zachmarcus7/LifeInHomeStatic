Get To Know Us

Nancy Shannon

Licensed Oregon Broker

At this point in my business, I find that many of my clients are homeowners making a lifestyle move - young families growing into their next home, single women enjoying ownership adventures, couples transitioning to new phases in their lives. I enjoy the whole process of helping them sell their current home and then finding the next perfect home.

I am a hard-working, committed business owner and Broker. Gratefully, my past clients have voted for me as one of Portland Magazine's "5 Star Realtors" every year since 2012 to 2022. I embrace my role as an advocate for my clients and work diligently to maintain an excellent reputation among my peers and industry affiliates. I have always sought careers that put me on the front line helping others. I chose real estate because HOME is one of the most powerful words in any language - and helping people find and enjoy the benefits of their own home builds a better community for all of us.
