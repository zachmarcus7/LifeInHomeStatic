Finding Your New Home

What to Know When Starting the Search for your New Home



When you start your search for a new home, an important first step is to realize what your main objectives are.

What kind of home are you looking for? What's motivating you to make a move?

With the Life in Home Team, we'll start off by figuring out the answers to these questions together. We'll help figure out what kind of home we should start searching for, and how we can best fulfill your end goals.

Finding Your New Home With Our Team



Figuring Out Your Ideal Home

Once your ideal home is determined, I will stay committed to finding it. Viewings will be scheduled regularly, where I will accompany you and offer advice on the current property. During this time, I will also present an analysis on current market trends, and whether or not a property you're interested is a worthwhile investment.


Negotiating Your Best Deal

Once a home is found that fits your requirements, I will actively negotiate to get you the best deal. Using decades of experience, I'll ensure contracts and transaction documents are precise and up-to-date. Along with contract expertise, I will also give referrals for Portland's best lenders, appraisers, and inspectors to guarantee you're getting the best possible deal for the home you want.

Above And Beyond

I will keep a constant lookout for prospective homes that align with your goals, ensuring that the homes I bring to your attention are ones that you'd be interested in while also being worth your time.

Get In Touch With Us


If you're curious about the process of selling your home or buying a new one, or if you just have a few general questions, please feel free to reach out. We're more than happy to provide any information to get you started in the right direction of finding your new home.
